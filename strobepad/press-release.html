Press Release

licht.team introduces StrobePad, a compact wireless strobe controller for fast and focused live operation

licht.team announces StrobePad, a compact handheld wireless strobe controller designed for direct control of a Stairville Power Strobe 1500 DMX via a compatible wireless DMX receiver.

StrobePad is built for simple, immediate operation in live and setup environments. The device features two dedicated rotary controls for flash frequency and intensity, a momentary Fire trigger button, and an integrated display for active settings and device status. Output remains safely off until triggered, allowing precise and predictable control during use.

Designed for portable operation, StrobePad offers local on-device configuration for DMX address, wireless ID, and wireless frequency/channel, with settings retained after power cycle. The unit is powered by a rechargeable battery system with USB-C charging and a user-replaceable 18650 battery concept.

With its compact format, focused feature set, and low-latency wireless operation, StrobePad is aimed at users who need a practical and reliable dedicated controller for strobe triggering, effects control, and fixture setup.

About licht.team licht.team develops practical lighting tools and control solutions with a focus on direct usability, compact form factors, and field-oriented design.
